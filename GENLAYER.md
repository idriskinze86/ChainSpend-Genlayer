# ChainSpend × GenLayer

## Vision

ChainSpend is a decentralized expense tracker that helps users record and manage expenses on-chain.

By integrating GenLayer's Intelligent Contracts, ChainSpend can evolve into an AI-powered financial assistant capable of reasoning about transactions instead of simply storing them.

---

# Why GenLayer?

Traditional smart contracts execute deterministic logic.

GenLayer introduces Intelligent Contracts that can reason over real-world information and make context-aware decisions.

This opens the door to smarter expense management.

---

# Proposed Features

## AI Expense Categorization

Instead of requiring users to manually select a category, an Intelligent Contract can analyze the transaction description.

Example:

Input:
Paid for lunch at KFC
Output:
Category: Food & Dining

---

## Duplicate Expense Detection

Before accepting a new expense, GenLayer can compare it with previous records.

Example:

Existing Expense

- Netflix
- $20
- July 18

New Submission

- Netflix
- $20
- July 18

Result

Potential duplicate detected.

---

## Fraud Detection

GenLayer can identify spending patterns that appear abnormal.

Example:

Normal spending:

- $20
- $40
- $15

New expense:

- $7,000

The Intelligent Contract flags the transaction for review.

---

## Reimbursement Dispute Resolution

Employees or DAO members can submit supporting evidence.

The Intelligent Contract evaluates:

- receipts
- timestamps
- descriptions
- participant claims

before recommending a fair outcome.

---

## Spending Insights

Future versions could generate insights such as:

- highest spending category
- monthly trends
- unusual spending behavior
- budgeting recommendations
