# Expense Court Workflow

## Step 1

User submits an expense.

↓

## Step 2

Another participant disputes it.

↓

## Step 3

Both upload evidence.

↓

## Step 4

GenLayer Intelligent Contract analyzes:

- receipts
- expense amount
- dates
- merchant
- user explanations

↓

## Step 5

AI reasoning generates a recommendation.

Possible outcomes:

- Approve reimbursement
- Reject reimbursement
- Split reimbursement
- Request additional evidence
